Answer: Oscar Nightmare

For this assignment, one of the first things to consider is the order of importance for each piece of information.

The original design had this order as:

  1. The Oscars logo
  2. Movie Title & Awardee
  3. Award Category.

A more logical order would be:

  1. Awardee
  2. Category
  3. Film Title
  4. Oscar Logo
