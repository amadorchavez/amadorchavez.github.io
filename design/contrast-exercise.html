Exercise 1.2

Oscar Nightmare

During the 2016 Academy Award ceremony there was a big snafu when Warren Beatty announced the winner of the Best Picture award as "La La Land." This wasn't the case and occurred partly because Beatty was handed the wrong envelope. Still, he ideally should have been able to recognize he was handed the wrong card. A contributing factor may have been the design of the card.

Here it is:

Using the principles of *contrast*, what would you do to redesign the card so it could be easier to understand the meaning it intends to communicate?

Please submit the following:

  1. A sketch of your redesign on paper or in PowerPoint
  2. A written explanation of your reasoning
