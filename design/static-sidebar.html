The Truth About Design

Learning a skill like design is more than just memorization. It's not "use this exact shade of red" or "just this one font." It's more than rules. It's developing a new way of seeing and understanding the world. It's a mindset shift. More than just memorizing rules.

When you're learning something, knowing what to skip is just as important as knowing what to study. We're going to skip what a lot of other courses dwell on.
