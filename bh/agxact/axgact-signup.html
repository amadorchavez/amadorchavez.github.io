What is Agxact?

Agxact is a web-based platform for managing farms. The platform includes a variety of user-friendly tools for tracking fields, planning crops, and logging expenses. Our goal is to help move the planning process from out of the farmer's head and into a manageable system. When you use Agxact, you will see your farm like you've never seen it before and expose new insights that have been hiding in plain sight.

Who is Agxact For?

Agxact was designed for:

  • Medium or large farms with 10+ fields
  • Farms that plant multiple crops every year
  • Farmers who are very hands-on with their farm
